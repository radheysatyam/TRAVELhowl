VISIT UTTAR PRADESH

Uttar Pradesh is known for its rich culture and tradition. It is home to Ayodhya and Mathura birthplace of Lord Rama, Lord Krishna and Bhgwan Parshuram respectively. Uttar Pradesh attracts many national and international tourists. Taj Mahal, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World in Agra is also located in Uttar Pradesh.

NAMMA KARNATAKA

By virtue of its varied geography and long history, Karnataka hosts numerous spots of interest for tourists. There is an array of ancient sculptured temples, modern cities, scenic hill ranges, forests and beaches. Karnataka has been ranked as the fourth most popular destination for tourism among the states of India..

TAMIL NADU

Tamil Nadu is India's largest tourism industry, accounting for 21.31% of domestic and 21.86% of foreign tourist visits. The state is a popular destination for beaches, relaxation, pilgrimage, hills and mountains, adventure, wildlife, trekking, food, and heritage.
